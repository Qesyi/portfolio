My journey

From discovering programming to where i am now.

  1. 2016 — 2018

    Discovered programming

    MRSM Lenggong

    At 15, a summer camp introduced me to Arduino — building a Bluetooth-controlled RC car from scratch. That spark of "I want to build things like this" became the goal. My PT3 results (8A's, 2B's) fell just short of perfect, but the direction was set.

  2. 2019 — 2020

    Set my sights on SPM

    MRSM Pasir Salak

    Changed schools with one focus: ace SPM — and it paid off with 9A's, opening the door to a scholarship abroad. Alongside schoolwork, I also picked up my first language, C++, teaching myself through YouTube tutorials and small side projects.

  3. 2021

    Began Software Engineering

    Moscow Aviation Institute

    Awarded a scholarship to study Software Engineering in Russia. This is where the real learning happened: the do's and don'ts, sleepless nights fixing bugs, and side projects outside the curriculum just to keep sharpening the craft.

  4. 2026

    Graduated — Software Engineering

    Moscow Aviation Institute

    Successfully completed my Bachelor's degree. Built plenty of "okay" projects along the way (a few still unfinished), and if nothing else, thoroughly mastered the art of talking to a computer.
